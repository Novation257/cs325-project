Join The NVIDIA Metropia 2042 Challenge To Re-Invent Cities Of The Future

Feb 28, 2019, 11:57am EST

In the 2018 film Black Panther, the Marvel Cinematic Universe introduced the masses to Wakanda, the world’s most technologically advanced nation. The utopian vision for its capital city perfectly blended culture and the warmth of humanity, with high technology — bringing us a more enlightened view into our future.
In the spirit of Wakanda’s original creators, NVIDIA’s Metropia 2042 ArtStation challenge proposes a future where technological advancements have benefited humanity — in a world surrounded by AI-assistants, robotics, autonomous vehicles, augmented reality and environmental protections.
From sustainable cities to innovative vehicles, everyone has a vision of what cutting-edge technology in the future will look like. And now, you have the opportunity to bring your vision to life.
Here’s your chance to design, 3D model and render an image to showcase on ArtStation, a platform where artists share their work and engage with others in their community.
There are four categories in this challenge:


Each category presents a chance to show off your 3D rendering skills and how you envision technology will take us to the future. Your work may even show up on the NVIDIA Creators Instagram.
Winners will be selected by a group of esteemed judges - creators themselves. NVIDIA has also teamed up with HP,  Adobe, Chaos Group, Otoy, Substance, and ZBrush to provide an amazing lineup of prizes for the winners.
Submissions are now open.
The deadline for entries is March 18, and winners will be announced on April 1.
Visit the ArtStation page to learn more about the NVIDIA Metropia 2042 challenge.
