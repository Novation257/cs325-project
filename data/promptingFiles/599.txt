Nvidia Is Giving Away A Super Limited Edition ‘CyberPunk 2077’ RTX 2080 Ti Graphics Card

Feb 17, 2020, 01:24pm EST

Nvidia and CD Projekt RED are running a contest with a pretty sweet prize: A very limited edition Nvidia GeForce 2080 Ti Cyberpunk 2077 special edition graphics card that isn’t even going up for sale (until winners hawk it on eBay, that is).
It’s a sweet looking card, canary yellow with the Cyberpunk 2077 logo on it. A 2080 Ti is a beast, as well, so you’ll be able to run just about anything out there flawlessly assuming you have a good enough system that doesn’t bottleneck your GPU.
In a blog post Nvidia jokingly renames this the RTX 2077 Ti.
Only 200 of these babies were produced so this is going to be an ultra-rare graphics card. Fans of the upcoming game from The Witcher 3 developer can win one by hopping on Twitter and following the steps in this tweet:
Want one? Here's how:
1. RT this video.
2. Tag a gamer who is as excited as you about Cyberpunk 2077 in the replies with #RTXOn
3. If selected, you BOTH win these limited edition GPUs!
You can also enter the contest on Nvidia’s Instagram or Facebook pages so you’re not locked to Twitter here.
The full terms and conditions for the contest can be found here.
No review units are being sent out for this card either so this is truly a very limited, very exclusive prize. I want one. I want to play Cyberpunk 2077 using this card in my gaming rig. But I never win anything and I probably can’t enter anyways since I’m writing about it. I will happily accept your winnings, however.
